# Short-Form Video Scripts

Use screen recording plus simple captions. Keep most videos under 35 seconds.

## Visual Checklist

- Start on the Bumi Money homepage.
- Load demo data.
- Show the projected balance changing.
- Show calendar view.
- Show stress test.
- End with URL.

## Script 1: The Core Pain

Hook:

"Budgeting apps told me where my money went. They did not tell me if I would overdraft next Thursday."

Steps:

1. Show current balance.
2. Add paycheck.
3. Add rent/bills.
4. Show future low-balance day.
5. Open calendar view.

CTA:

"I made this free. Search Bumi or use the link."

## Script 2: Paycheck-to-Paycheck Calendar

Hook:

"If your money life revolves around payday, this is the view budgeting apps should have had."

Steps:

1. Load demo plan.
2. Click Calendar.
3. Point to paychecks and bills.
4. Point to lowest balance day.

CTA:

"Free, no signup, local-only."

## Script 3: Job Loss Scenario

Hook:

"How many days could you survive if income stopped?"

Steps:

1. Open Scenarios.

3. Show updated year-end balance.
4. Click Stress Test My Life.

"This is planning, not panic."
