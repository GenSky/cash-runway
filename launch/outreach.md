# Outreach

Do not mass-send. Personalize every message.

## Creator Targets

Look for small and mid-sized creators who talk about:

- Paycheck-to-paycheck planning
- Debt payoff
- Budgeting for beginners
- Frugal living
- Cash stuffing
- Low income finance
- Single parent budgeting
- Job loss preparedness
- Balance transfers
- Car affordability

Target profile:

- 2,000 to 100,000 followers
- Active comments
- Practical tone, not get-rich-quick
- Willing to share free tools

Avoid:

- Payday loan promoters
- High-pressure credit repair accounts
- Crypto/speculative finance accounts
- Creators whose content conflicts with "not financial advice"

## Search Queries

Use these on TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Substack, and Google:

- "paycheck budget"
- "budget with me paycheck"
- "debt free journey"
- "cash stuffing paycheck"
- "low income budget"
- "single mom budget"
- "how to avoid overdraft"
- "balance transfer payoff"
- "car payment budget"
- "emergency fund beginner"
