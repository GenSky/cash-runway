# Bumi Money Launch Kit

This folder contains the launch operating system for Bumi Money.

Goal: spread a free, no-signup cash-flow forecasting tool through useful demos, community feedback, creator outreach, and search-friendly content.

Core positioning:

> Bumi Money is a free tool that shows your future bank balance before your bills hit. It is not a budgeting app. No categories. No pie charts. No signup. Just future cash-flow forecasting.

Primary URL:

https://bumi.money/

## Launch Principles

- Lead with the user's pain: "Will I go negative before payday?"
- Be transparent that this is a personal/free project.
- Ask for feedback before asking for attention.
- Do not mass-post the same copy across communities.
- Do not imply financial advice. Use "planning tool" and "forecast" language.
- Do not post in any subreddit, group, or forum until its rules are checked.
- Track responses and improve the product based on repeated feedback.

## Files

- `launch-plan.md`: Phased launch strategy and weekly calendar.
- `post-drafts.md`: Draft posts for HN, Reddit-style communities, LinkedIn, X, Indie Hackers, and Product Hunt.
- `video-scripts.md`: Short-form video ideas and scripts.
- `outreach.md`: Creator, newsletter, and community outreach templates.
- `approval-checklist.md`: Required checks before anything is posted publicly.
- `metrics.md`: Simple metrics dashboard and learning loop.

## Operating Rule

Codex can prepare and coordinate. The owner approves public posting.
