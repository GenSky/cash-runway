## Indie Hackers

Title:

I built a free cash-flow forecast tool because budgeting apps answer the wrong question

Body:

I built Bumi Money as a free static PWA. The product is a forecast engine for future bank balances.

The problem:

Budgeting apps are often backward-looking. They show categories, charts, and past spending. But many people need a forward-looking answer:

> If my paycheck, mortgage, utilities, debt payments, and surprise expenses hit, what will my account balance be on a future date?

Current features:

- Income and expense events
- Weekly, biweekly, monthly, yearly, and one-time recurrence
- Daily event-day forecast
- Calendar view
- Monthly/yearly summaries
- Scenario center
- Stress tests
- Debt/car/emergency fund calculators
- Import/export JSON
- PWA/offline support
- No backend, no signup

I am keeping it free for now and trying to learn whether people understand the workflow quickly.

Feedback I want:

- Is the positioning clear?
- Would "cash-flow forecast" or "future balance calendar" make more sense?
- What would make this trustworthy enough to use every week?

URL:

https://bumi.money/
