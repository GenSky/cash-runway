Summary

Bumi Money is designed as a local-first planning tool. The app stores your balance, forecast events, theme, and settings in your browser storage on your device.

Data Stored By The App

The current version stores plan data in LocalStorage under the key cash-runway-v1. This can include balances, event names, amounts, dates, recurrence settings, notes, and display settings.

Data We Do Not Collect

The current static app does not use accounts, servers, databases, analytics, advertising pixels, or payment processing. Your saved plan is not sent to Bumi Money by the app.

Exports And Device Storage

If you export a JSON file, that file is created in your browser and saved wherever your device or browser stores downloads. Anyone with access to that file may be able to read the plan data inside it.

Browser Controls

You can remove saved plan data by using Reset Data in the app or by clearing site data for this website in your browser settings.

Future Changes

If Bumi Money adds accounts, payments, sync, analytics, or other hosted features, this policy should be updated before those features are launched.
